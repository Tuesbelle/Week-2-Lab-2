About Tu es Belle

Our Vision

To be a sanctuary of nourishment and well-being, where every guest feels valued and inspired to embrace a healthier lifestyle. Through fresh, flavorful, and thoughtfully curated meals, we strive to foster a dining experience that promotes balance, connection, and joy.

Our Mission

At Tu es Belle, our mission is to provide fresh, nutritious, and delicious meals and beverages that cater to diverse dietary preferences—welcoming vegans, meat-lovers, and health-conscious individuals alike. We are committed to using high-quality ingredients, embracing sustainability, and creating an elegant yet comforting environment where wellness meets indulgence. Through exceptional service, wholesome offerings, and a dedication to health, we aim to empower our guests to nourish their bodies and uplift their spirits.

Our Story

Tu es Belle was born from a passion for health, wellness, and a deep love for delicious, wholesome food. Founded by Cassandra Moore, the restaurant is a reflection of her journey towards a balanced lifestyle—one that embraces both indulgence and nutrition. Tu es Belle is more than just a place to eat; it’s a community dedicated to helping individuals discover the joy of nourishing their bodies while enjoying every bite.


A Message from Cassandra Moore

"Welcome to Tu es Belle! My dream has always been to create a space where people can enjoy food that is as nourishing as it is delicious. Whether you're here for a refreshing juice, a comforting meal, or a moment of relaxation with herbal tea, I hope you feel inspired to embrace wellness in your own way. You are beautiful, inside and out."

"Let food be thy medicine and medicine be thy food." – Hippocrates
